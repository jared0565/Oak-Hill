Cookie policy

This site sets no tracking cookies unless you say yes. Here is exactly what can be stored on your device and how to change your mind. Last updated 13 June 2026.

Strictly necessary

Always on. One small entry in your browser's local storage remembers the cookie choices you made, so we do not ask you on every visit.

NameProviderPurposeLasts
ohpc-consentThis siteStores your cookie choicesUntil you clear it

Analytics

Off by default. If you allow analytics, we use Google Analytics 4 to count visits and see which pages help people, so we can improve the site. These cookies are set only after you consent.

NameProviderPurposeLasts
_gaGoogleTells repeat visitors apart13 months
_ga_*GoogleKeeps count of visits per session13 months

Advertising

Off by default, and not currently used. If we ever run ads to tell local families about the cafe, Google Ads conversion cookies would only be set with your consent, and this page will list them first.

Embedded services

The calendar page shows a Google Calendar (events, party slots and closures) and the contact page shows a Google Map of the cafe. These load from Google when you open those pages, and Google may set its own cookies under Google's privacy policy. The rest of the site sets no Google cookies.

Changing your mind

Use the link (also in the footer of every page) to change your choices at any time. You can also delete cookies through your browser settings; the site keeps working either way.
