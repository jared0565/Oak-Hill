Cookie policy

This site sets no tracking cookies unless you say yes. Here is exactly what can be stored on your device and how to change your mind. Last updated 13 June 2026.

Strictly necessary

Always on. One small entry in your browser's local storage remembers the cookie choices you made, so we do not ask you on every visit.

NameProviderPurposeLasts
ohpc-consentThis siteStores your cookie choicesUntil you clear it

Counting visits (no cookies)

We count anonymous page visits to see which pages help families. This uses no cookies and stores nothing on your device, and we keep no IP address or anything that identifies you — so it needs no consent. The optional Google Analytics described below is separate and only runs if you allow it.

Analytics

Off by default. If you allow analytics, we use Google Analytics 4 to count visits and see which pages help people, so we can improve the site. These cookies are set only after you consent.

NameProviderPurposeLasts
_gaGoogleTells repeat visitors apart13 months
_ga_*GoogleKeeps count of visits per session13 months

Analytics & advertising tags

Off by default. With your consent we may run optional analytics or advertising tags to understand which pages help families and to measure whether our local ads work. These are managed by us and load only after you allow the matching category. Depending on what we're running, they may include tags from Google, Meta (Facebook Pixel), Microsoft, TikTok, LinkedIn or similar providers, each under its own privacy policy. You can change or withdraw your choice any time via .

Embedded services

The calendar page (events, party slots and closures) is served by us and loads nothing from Google. The contact page can show a Google Map of the cafe, but only after you choose to load it — until you press Show map, nothing loads from Google and no Google cookies are set. If you do load the map, Google may set its own cookies under Google's privacy policy. The rest of the site sets no Google cookies.

Changing your mind

Use the link (also in the footer of every page) to change your choices at any time. You can also delete cookies and site data (including the small choice we store) through your browser settings; the site keeps working either way.
